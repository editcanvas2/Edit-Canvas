Security at EditPDF

Your security is not just a feature; it is the foundation of our entire platform. We designed EditPDF from the ground up with a "privacy-first" architecture that puts you in complete control of your documents.

Client-Side Processing: Your Files Stay Yours

The single most important security feature of EditPDF is that **your files are never uploaded to our servers.** All the processing—merging, splitting, rotating, and protecting—happens directly on your own device within your web browser. This is known as client-side processing.

How It Works:

  1. When you select a file, it is loaded directly into your browser's memory. It does not travel over the internet.
  2. Our tools, written in JavaScript, access and manipulate the file data locally on your computer.
  3. When you download the finished file, it is saved directly from your browser to your computer's storage.

The Benefits:

  • Zero Data Exposure: Because your files never leave your device, there is zero risk of them being intercepted or accessed by anyone else, including us.
  • No Data Retention: We do not, and technically cannot, store your files. Once you close your browser tab, the file data is gone from memory.
  • **Complete Confidentiality:** Your sensitive personal, financial, or business documents remain confidential, always under your control.

Secure Connections (HTTPS)

Our website is served to your browser over an encrypted HTTPS connection. This means all communication between your browser and our server (to load the application code itself) is secure and cannot be tampered with. This protects you from man-in-the-middle attacks and ensures the integrity of the tools you are using.

If you have any further questions about our security practices, please do not hesitate to contact us.
