Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

At EditPDF, we take your privacy seriously. Our philosophy is simple: your files are your files. We have designed our service so that we never have access to your documents.

1. No File Uploads, No Data Collection

EditPDF operates entirely within your web browser. When you use our tools, you are opening a local file on your computer, and our JavaScript code processes it directly on your machine. **Your PDF files are never sent to, uploaded to, or stored on our servers.** This architecture provides the highest level of privacy possible, as there is no data for us to collect or for any third party to intercept.

2. Usage Analytics

To improve our website and understand which tools are most popular, we may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data. This data is non-personal and cannot be used to identify you or your files. For example, we might track how many times the "Merge PDF" tool is used in a day, but we will never know who used it or what files they merged.

3. Cookies

EditPDF does not use cookies to track you. We may use local storage in your browser for essential site functionality, such as remembering a setting, but never for tracking your activity across other sites.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with third-party services that would compromise your privacy. Our website is self-contained to ensure your data stays on your device.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us.
