About EditPDF

In a world where data privacy is more important than ever, we noticed a problem: most online PDF tools require you to upload your sensitive documents to a third-party server. You have no control over who sees your files or how long they are stored. We believed there had to be a better way.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide powerful, simple, and completely private PDF tools that are accessible to everyone, for free. We are committed to building a service where you never have to choose between convenience and security.

Our Technology

We leverage the power of modern web browsers and cutting-edge JavaScript libraries to perform all PDF manipulations directly on your computer. This client-side approach means your files are never sent over the internet. What you do on EditPDF stays on your device, period.

Our Promise

  • Privacy First: We will never ask you to upload your files.
  • Always Free: Core PDF tools should be accessible to everyone, without subscriptions.
  • Simplicity: Our tools are designed to be intuitive and easy to use, with no unnecessary clutter.

Thank you for choosing EditPDF. We hope our tools make your life a little bit easier.
